I'm a first-year Cyber Security Engineering student who got hooked on the idea that every system, physical or digital, has a story about trust: who it lets in, who it keeps out, and why. That's what pulled me toward security — and it's the same curiosity that pushes me into AI, IoT and web development, because none of those fields make sense in isolation anymore.

I like problems that don't have a clean textbook answer. Hackathons are where that shows up best — a short clock, a real constraint, and a team figuring it out together. I bring the same energy to sports: quick decisions, reading the play, backing my teammates.

Outside of coursework, I'm usually deep in a side project, a CTF, or a rabbit hole about how something I use every day actually works under the hood.
